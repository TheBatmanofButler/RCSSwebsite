Your support of the Research Cluster on Science and Subjectivity helps us to fulfill our mission to reconsider the large question - is the natural normative? - from both scientific and subjective perspectives, and to examine the social, medical and political implications of our current inability to reach a single answer. Donations to the RCSS will help us to bring innovative new programs, seminars, speakers and projects to you through our interdisciplinary, inter-school, collaborative forum.
If you have any questions about donations or would like more information about how you can help support the CSSR, please email the Director, Professor Robert Pollack.
